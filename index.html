About Us

The Students’ Chapter CSE is a large community with over 200 members .
Our community has members who are experts in various fields such as web development ,app development and other fields.
We have been a community partner at Hack4Bengal 2.0 and helped in 200+ reginstrations.
The IEI Students’ Chapter aims for maximum participation from the members of the community and the students of the college.It aims for enhancing the interest of students in coding.
It is going to be helpful for the students in the long run as they can develop their problem solving skills through this hackathon.
Through this hackathon, they can learn the importance of problem solving and teamwork.

PAST EVENTS

TECHQUISITIVE

TECHQUISITIVE: The inaugural offline event by Students’ Chapter CSE, boasting a record-breaking 700+ participants, making it AOT's largest-ever event. This captivating quiz contest saw diverse teams from various streams engage in a lively and enjoyable competition. Winners received prize money and swag, while all participants were treated to food.

STRIDE

STRIDE: A 3-week seminar/webinar series organized by the Students’ Chapter CSE at Academy of Technology, attracting over 300 participants. Notable alumni and industry professionals delivered insightful talks on topics including Cloud Computing, SDE preparation, Data Science, international internships like MITACS, GATE readiness, SWE preparation, and more.

TECHNOCRUISE

TECHNOCRUISE: One of the largest tech events by Students’ Chapter CSE, AOT, in collaboration with IIT Kanpur. Over 200 students participated, with seminars on Ethical Hacking and IoT by renowned professors. An IARC and I.I.I zonal competition gave winners a shot at Techkriti, IIT Kanpur's renowned Annual Tech Fest.

FAQs

What is a Hackathon?
A Hackathon is a social coding event where programmers, designers, and developers collaborate to solve a porblem and compete for cash prizes. It's one part party, one part work-hard overnight battle against the clock and the competition
Why should I participate?
This is the first ever Hackathon in Academy of Technology.You can participate for a lot of reason: the challenge, the creative outlet, the community collaboration, the networking, the swags and maybe Prizes!
Can I participate remotely?
InterHacktive is a Hybrid mode hackathon. Here the first round will be online but, the final presentation must be shown in person.
How much does it cost to participate?
Nothing, participation in InterHacktive is totally FREE!!
What is the maximum/ minimum team size?
We encourage you to make a team of minimum 2 and maximum 4 members. If you are a solo Hacker and do not have a team, you can still register and we will help you find a team.
Who can participate?
This Hackathon is Intra College,so only Students from Academy Of Technology can participate.
